    One-dimensional wave propagation

    \begin{align} \rho \frac{\partial^2 \delta}{\partial t^2} = \frac{\partial}{\partial x} \left( \mu \frac{\partial \delta}{\partial x} \right) + f \end{align}

    \begin{align} \rho \frac{\partial v}{\partial t} &= \frac{\partial \tau}{\partial x} + f \\ \frac{\partial \tau}{\partial t} &= \mu \frac{\partial v}{\partial x} \\ v &= \frac{\partial \delta}{\partial t} \end{align}
