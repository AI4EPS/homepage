    Single degree of freedom spring-slider system

    (P333, Segal 2010)

    \begin{align} \tau &=\tau_0 - n V - \kappa \delta \\ &= \frac{\mu}{2c} \\ \kappa &= \frac{2\mu}{\pi L} \\ \end{align}

    $$ \tau_{lock} = \tau_0 - \kappa \delta \\ $$ $$ \tau_{str}(\delta)= \begin{cases}\tau_p-\left(\tau_p-\tau_r\right) \delta / D_c, & \delta \le D_c \\ \tau_r & \delta>D_c\end{cases} $$

    \begin{align} f &= f_s - (f_s - f_d) * \min(\delta, D_c) / D_c \nonumber \\ \tau_{str} &= f \cdot _n \nonumber \end{align}
